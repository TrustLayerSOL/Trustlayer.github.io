# TrustLayer Market Research

Research snapshot: April 29, 2026.

## Landscape

Solana meme coin launch products are converging around creator fees, deployer
rewards, and fast launch UX. The important gap is not whether fees exist. The
gap is whether traders believe the fee recipient, fee flow, and reward mechanics
are legitimate.

## Competitor Notes

### pump.fun

Strengths:

- Default mindshare.
- Fastest launch habit for most traders.
- Deepest liquidity and social gravity.
- Creator fee model already teaches the market that fees can be routed.

Weaknesses:

- Extreme churn.
- Bot/sniper environment.
- Trust is mostly social, not systematic.
- Many launches feel extractive.

### Bonk/LetsBONK

Strengths:

- Ecosystem alignment.
- Deployer rewards and BONK narrative.
- Raydium liquidity connection.

Weaknesses:

- Fee model changes can confuse users.
- Less dominant than pump.fun.
- Trust depends on broader ecosystem reputation.

### Bags-style creator royalties

Strengths:

- Creator monetization is simple to understand.
- Claimed royalties can be shown publicly.
- Social/creator angle is strong.

Weaknesses:

- Unauthorized creator tokens create trust issues.
- Royalties can look like creator extraction.
- Holder benefit is not always obvious.

### Raydium LaunchLab

Strengths:

- Serious infrastructure.
- Configurable launch mechanics.
- Real AMM integration.

Weaknesses:

- More complex than casual meme launch UX.
- Distribution/mindshare is harder than pump.fun.

### BLIMP-like tools

Strengths:

- Shows demand for creator fees being converted into buybacks, airdrops, or holder rewards.
- Simple story: fees go back to holders.

Weaknesses:

- New/small projects need proof of custody, automation, and payout history.
- If dashboard disappears, trust evaporates.

## TrustLayer Wedge

TrustLayer should own:

- Creator legitimacy.
- Fee transparency.
- Reward quality.
- Protection reserve visibility.
- Revocable verification.
- Post-launch accountability.

Positioning line:

```text
TrustLayer proves where launch fees go and whether holder rewards are real.
```

## Product Opportunity

Launchpads want speed and volume. Traders want proof. Developers want a trust
signal that helps a launch convert attention into buyers.

TrustLayer can become neutral infrastructure:

- Embeddable badge.
- Token dashboard.
- Public API.
- Project directory.
- Verification history.

The first version should support existing launch flows rather than trying to
replace them.
