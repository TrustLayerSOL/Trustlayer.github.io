# Meme Coin Research And Concepts

This is cultural/product research for meme design and community behavior, not a
recommendation to buy, sell, coordinate trading, or make return claims.

## Common Patterns

- Names are ultra-short and culturally familiar. Recent Solana/X traffic keeps
  rewarding slang or obvious reaction words like UNC, BULL, LOL, UNKNOWN, WOULD,
  USELESS, CHILLGUY, and FARTCOIN. The meme has to be understandable before
  anyone explains the token.
- Tickers are plain, loud, and screenshot-friendly. Usually 3-8 characters, all
  caps, easy to type in an X reply, and funny when used as a sentence.
- Simple mascot beats complex lore. UNC works because "unc energy" is a broad
  social role: calm older guy, reaction image, easy captions, and endless remix
  potential.
- Launch narratives are minimal: fair launch, no presale, community-driven,
  Pump.fun/Solana, then visibility events.
- X behavior is reply-native. The winning loop is templates, quote-post jokes,
  chart screenshots, PFP adoption, and community replies under larger crypto
  accounts.
- Durability comes from remixable identity, not only price. The strongest memes
  have one recognizable visual or character posture that can be redrawn,
  captioned, made into a PFP, or inserted into current-event formats.

## Risks / Anti-Patterns

- Do not promise gains, "next 100x," guaranteed listings, insider access, fake
  utility, burns, or charity outcomes.
- Avoid celebrity likenesses, copyrighted characters, or "official" implications
  unless rights are real.
- Avoid botty raid language, artificial volume, fake screenshots, or coordinated
  manipulation.
- Do not overbuild lore. If the joke needs a whitepaper, it is probably too
  heavy.
- Avoid art that is too polished to remix. The best meme assets feel copyable in
  30 seconds.
- Do not rely only on a listing catalyst. CEX visibility can create volatility,
  but culture has to stand without it.

## Concepts

### $RECEIPTS

Premise: The TrustLayer proof coin. It turns the question "where did the fees
go?" into a meme.

Visual style: simple receipt printer, red X versus green check, "trust me" versus
"show me," dev fee bag versus public receipts.

First posts:

1. Where are the fees going?
2. Ask for receipts.
3. "Trust me" is not a launch plan.

Why remixable: Receipt paper can list anything. People can caption it with fee
flow, airdrops, missing promises, or public proof.

### $LOADING

Premise: The universal state of being stuck at 99%. Everyone is waiting for
something: the chart, the reply, the transaction, or the weekend.

Visual style: a dead-simple loading spinner with tired eyes. White/black base,
one accent color. Works as icon, sticker, PFP, or overlay on screenshots.

First posts:

1. still loading.
2. Image: spinner at 99%. Caption: "me waiting for my plan to become a strategy"
3. Template drop: "put the spinner on anything that is taking too long"

Why remixable: The spinner can be pasted onto charts, apps, group chats,
relationships, job applications, or market narratives.

### $UNREAD

Premise: The red notification badge that keeps growing. Anxiety, alpha, ignored
DMs, group chats, unread announcements.

Visual style: a red app badge with a face inside it. Numbers change: 1, 7, 99+,
404, "soon."

First posts:

1. you have 99+ unread signals
2. Image: red badge over a chart. Caption: "opened the app and chose peace"
3. reply with what your unread count is actually hiding

Why remixable: The badge is a universal overlay. The community can put it on
wallets, X accounts, calendars, exchange apps, or life screenshots.

### $BAGWORK

Premise: Not "bags" as profit hype, but the daily labor of carrying everything:
groceries, bad decisions, group chat drama, and market stress.

Visual style: a plain grocery bag with little arms, tired posture, and label
space on the front. Meme template: write anything on the bag.

First posts:

1. Image: tired bag. Caption: "carrying the timeline again"
2. what's in your bag today?
3. Template drop: blank bag PNG with caption "community asset, ruin responsibly"

Why remixable: The front of the bag is a blank canvas. People can label it
"taxes," "SOL fees," "unanswered DMs," "delusion," "Monday," etc.
